# Business Event Matrix Adds a Notes Field

**27 March 2026**

AgileDataGuides today released a free-form Notes field for domains and concepts in the Business Event Matrix, giving teams a place to capture the context that doesn't fit anywhere else.

## The Problem

The matrix had structured fields for everything important — name, description, aliases, owner, W's category — but no place for the messy stuff. Every team has notes that don't fit a structured field: open questions ("is this Customer the same as the Customer in the loyalty programme?"), pending decisions ("agreed with Sales we'll fold Patron into Customer next quarter"), and history ("originally split out by region; merged 2023-08").

## The Solution

The Domain Details and Concept Details modals now include a multi-line **Notes** textarea. Notes appear in the hover tooltip alongside description, aliases, and owner — making them visible without opening the modal.

## Key Benefits

- **Unstructured context** — captures the why, the open questions, and the history
- **Visible at hover** — read notes in two seconds without opening the modal
- **Saved with the matrix** — notes persist alongside everything else in the JSON file
- **Identical for both** — same Notes field on domains and concepts

The Notes field is available now in the latest release.
