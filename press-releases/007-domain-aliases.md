# Domain Aliases

**Date:** 2026-03-24

## What's New

- **Domain Aliases** — Domains can now have aliases, just like concepts. For example, "Sales" might also be known as "Revenue" or "Commerce".

- **Aliases in Domain Tooltips** — Hovering over a domain header shows its aliases as pill badges below the description.

- **Unified Aliases Editing** — The "Edit details" modal now shows the aliases field for both domains and concepts. Enter comma-separated alternative names and save.
