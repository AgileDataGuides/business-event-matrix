# Concept Aliases

**Date:** 2026-03-24

## What's New

- **Concept Aliases** — Concepts can now have multiple aliases (alternative names). For example, "Customer" might also be known as "Client", "Buyer", or "Patron".

- **Aliases in Tooltips** — Hovering over a concept header shows its aliases as small pill badges below the description.

- **Edit Aliases** — The "Edit details" modal for concepts now includes an aliases field where you enter comma-separated alternative names. The aliases are parsed, trimmed, and stored as an array.

- **Example Data** — The Ice Cream Shop example has been updated with sample aliases for all concepts (e.g. Product: Item, SKU, Flavour).
