# Change Concept W's

**Date:** 2026-03-24

## What's New

- **Editable W's** — You can now change the W's (Who, What, Where, When, How) of an existing concept. Open the "Edit details" modal by hovering over a concept header and clicking "Edit details", then use the W's dropdown to reassign the concept to a different category. The concept will move to the correct column group in the matrix on save.
